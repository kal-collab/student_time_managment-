StudyLife — Student Time Manager

StudyLife is a smart student productivity website designed to help students balance every part of life — class schedules, study sessions, enjoyment, and friendships. It offers task management, daily balance tracking, mood check-ins, weekly planning, and focus timers to support academic success while maintaining personal well-being.

Features
📚 Class Time Management
✏️ Study Session Planner
🎮 Enjoyment & Break Tracking
🤝 Friendship & Social Time Balance
📅 Weekly Planner
😊 Daily Mood Check
⏱️ Focus Timer
💡 Productivity Tips
Purpose

To help students create a healthier, more organized, and balanced lifestyle by managing academics, personal growth, and social life in one platform.
